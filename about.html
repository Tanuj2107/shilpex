About US

We undertake our quest of improving the quality of human life with enthusiasm and vigour. Our vision for the future is to bridge the gap between patients and healthcare professionals through quality and affordable products. It finds purpose and direction with our strategic intent. It is guided by our corporate culture that places people and capabilities as the pivot that changes and transforms situations

Our Vision

"Our vision is to be a leading pharmaceutical company in India and to become a significant player offering differentiated products, serving unmet healthcare needs with affordable and quality medicines .We shall seek joint ventures with partners who are major players in the country or region. We aspire to enrich people lives through quality and innovative pharmaceuticals. By the turn of this decade, we shall be amongst the most admired companies in India."

Our Mission

Our Mission is to Enriching lives with quality and affordable pharmaceuticals. "We strive for a happier, healthier tomorrow.
